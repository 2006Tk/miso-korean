The Art Of Traditional Korean Gathering

At **MISO**, we believe dining is a communal ritual. Our tables are designed with premium, smokeless tabletop charcoal grills, allowing you to sear your meats to juicy perfection alongside friends, family, or colleagues.

Our kitchen is led by chefs trained in traditional Korean techniques. Every meal begins with a rich array of complimentary house-made side dishes (Banchan), including freshly fermented napa cabbage kimchi, seasoned spinach, and pickled radish.

Premium BBQ Selections

Experience premium cuts of meat prepared live at your table, served with fresh lettuce wraps (Ssam) and signature dipping sauces.

Samgyeopsal (삼겹살)

Thick strips of high-grade pork belly grilled to crisp tenderness, served with sesame oil salt and ssamjang paste.

Beef Bulgogi (소불고기)

Thinly sliced prime beef ribeye marinated in a savory soy-sesame glaze, grilled with sweet onions and garlic cloves.

Galbi (소갈비)

Butterflied short ribs marinated in fresh pear puree, honey, garlic, and soy sauce, resulting in a sweet and caramelized grill finish.
